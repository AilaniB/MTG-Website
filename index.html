Who Are We?


Welcome to UNC Charlotte's Magic the Gathering Club. We are an student lead organization that exists to connect students from all walks off life into the exciting world of Magic the Gathering. If you're interested in Commander, Cube, Limited, Standard, Pinoneer, and many other formats we're the place to find it. All skill levels are welcome and we encourage you to reach out on our discord and connect on NinerEngage for any questions.
